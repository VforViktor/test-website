Sales Enablement — Why Frequency Matters 02 / 03

Frequency Builds Trust

Audience penetration above 100% means decision-makers have seen Flexecharge multiple times. Your SDRs aren't cold-calling — they're following up.

1.87×
Controlled frequency
Each professional in our target audience has seen Flexecharge ads an average of 1.87 times — enough to build recognition, not enough to cause fatigue (sweet spot: 1.5–2.5×).

What does >100% mean? When penetration exceeds 100%, users in the segment have seen the ad more than once on average. For high-intent bottom-funnel audiences, this controlled repetition reinforces intent and primes purchase decisions.

What this means for Sales

  • When an SDR reaches out, the prospect has already seen Flexecharge — the brand is not new to them.
  • Bottom-funnel visitors (338 site visits) have been retargeted from awareness campaigns — they have context on the product before your call.
  • Higher familiarity shortens the trust-building phase of the sales cycle — spend less time on "who are you?" and more on "here's the business case."
  • The 0.32% CTR (+19%) shows the message is resonating — prospects are clicking on their own terms, not just landing cold.
