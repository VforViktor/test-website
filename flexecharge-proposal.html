Creative Testing Framework

From ad idea to
winner in 4 weeks.

Instead of guessing which creative works best, we run a structured sprint model that isolates one variable at a time. After 3 sprints, you have a creative playbook that compounds — each winner informs the next test.

Week 1–2 · Launch

Sprint: Test the angle

  • Deploy 4 ad variants with identical format but different copy angles (cost pain, aspiration, compliance reframe, social proof)
  • Minimum DKK 400/day budget per variant to reach significance
  • Measure: CTR, engagement rate, CPL
  • Kill the 2 weakest performers at Day 10
Week 3–4 · Optimise

Sprint: Test the format

  • Take the winning angle — now test the format: single image vs carousel vs video still
  • Keep copy identical to isolate the format variable
  • Measure: scroll depth, secondary CTA clicks, demo book rate
  • Winning format becomes the new control creative
Month 2 · Refine

Sprint: Test the visual hook

  • Lock angle + format. Now test the visual — photography vs illustration vs bold type treatment
  • FlexeCharge has strong real-world photography (Q8, Electra sites). Test against graphic/typographic styles
  • By Month 2 you have a proven angle, format, and visual combination
Month 3+ · Scale

The compounding library

  • Every winner is documented in a Creative Angle Library — a growing asset of proven concepts
  • New campaigns start from the library, not from scratch — dramatically reducing production time
  • New angles are tested against the existing champion, not against each other
  • The system gets smarter every month
